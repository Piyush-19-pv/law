Admission Open 2024-25

Aggarwal Law College of Women invites aspiring students to apply for the following integrated law courses:

B.A. LL.B. (Bachelor of Arts & Bachelor of Laws)

This 5-year integrated course combines the study of law with subjects of arts like Political Science, Sociology, etc.

LL.B. (Bachelor of Legislative Law)

This 3-year integrated course merges commerce education with legal studies, preparing students for careers in corporate law.

B.B.A. LL.B. (Bachelor of Business Administration & Bachelor of Laws)

A 5-year course focusing on business administration and law, ideal for students aiming to work in corporate legal departments.
