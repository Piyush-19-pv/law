Computer Lab & Library

Computer Lab

The Computer Lab at Aggarwal College of Law is designed with modern technology, offering students high-speed internet, latest software, and dedicated IT support for a superior learning experience.

Lab Facilities

  • Latest desktop computers with powerful processors and ample storage.
  • High-speed internet access for research and online learning.
  • Software for legal research, including case management systems and databases.
  • Printers, scanners, and other peripherals for student use.
  • Technical support staff available for assistance.

Library

The library provides access to extensive legal texts, journals, and digital resources. It also features a quiet study environment, group study rooms, and access to online legal databases.

Facilities

  • Extensive collection of textbooks, reference materials, and academic journals.
  • High-speed internet access for research and academic work.
  • Dedicated reading spaces with comfortable seating and ambient lighting.
  • Access to digital archives, e-books, and academic databases.
  • Group study rooms with projection facilities for collaborative learning.
