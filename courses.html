Programs Offered at Aggrawal Law College

B.A. LL.B. (Bachelor of Arts & Bachelor of Laws)

Duration: 5 years (10 semesters)

Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST candidates).

Overview: The B.A. LL.B. program integrates the study of law with humanities subjects like Political Science, Sociology, and History.

Key Highlights:

  • Comprehensive understanding of law with subjects like Constitutional Law, Criminal Law, and International Law.
  • Moot court participation, legal aid clinics, and internships.
  • Preparation for careers in litigation, corporate law, and civil services.

Career Opportunities:

  • Lawyer/Advocate
  • Judicial Services
  • Legal Advisor
  • Public Prosecutor

LL.B. ( Bachelor of Legislative Law)

Duration: 3 years (10 semesters)

Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST candidates).

Overview: The LL.B. program combines law with commerce, offering a foundation in business and legal principles.

Key Highlights:

  • Business Law, Taxation Law, Corporate Law, and Financial Management.
  • Legal aspects of contracts, mergers & acquisitions, and intellectual property rights.
  • Practical training through internships in corporate firms and financial institutions.

Career Opportunities:

  • Corporate Lawyer
  • Tax Advisor
  • Corporate Compliance Officer

B.B.A. LL.B. (Bachelor of Business Administration & Bachelor of Laws)

Duration: 5 years (10 semesters)

Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST candidates).

Overview: The B.B.A. LL.B. program blends law with business administration, preparing students for corporate law careers.

Key Highlights:

  • Business Management, Corporate Governance, and Human Resource Management.
  • Focus on corporate law, arbitration, mergers, and international business law.
  • Internships, moot court exercises, and business law seminars.

Career Opportunities:

  • Corporate Lawyer
  • Legal Officer in Corporate Sector
  • Management Consultant
